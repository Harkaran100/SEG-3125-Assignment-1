My Approach

How I Work

My design process starts with understanding the user, the task, and the purpose of the interface. I then create a simple structure, focus on visual hierarchy, choose colors and typography intentionally, and refine the design through feedback and iteration.

Since I am currently learning UI/UX design in SEG3125, this portfolio will evolve as I apply concepts such as user-centered design, accessibility, heuristic evaluation, and usability testing.

Step 01

Understand

Research the user's goals, context, and pain points before touching any design tools.

Step 02

Structure

Build a clear information hierarchy with wireframes and layout sketches before visual design.

Step 03

Design

Apply intentional color, typography, spacing, and contrast to create a polished visual interface.

Step 04

Iterate

Gather feedback, test usability, and continuously refine the design to better serve user needs.
